Fall Follies Falling
Dear Ladies and Gentleman,
There has been a plague which has ripped through our nation. Small boys and girls everywhere have plummeted to the concrete ground on Halloween night for years. This epidemic level issue deserves to be addressed rather than met with weak-willed acceptance. It is for our small trick or treat friends that I present to you these crucial solutions which would protect 100% of children from the lurking dangers of cracked sidewalks, poor visibility, and thoughtlessness.
The culprits of the bruises and scrapes found on children across the globe: sidwalks made of concrete instead of cotton candy, a capitalist society which doesn't support the wide distribution of glowsticks to all people, and kids being too young to stand upright. If you continue on to the gallery page, you'll find pictures of things which are wrong, ways to improve, and other things, which I am adventerously labeling as relevant.
