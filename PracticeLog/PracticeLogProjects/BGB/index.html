The Restaurant

The Black Goose Bistro offers casual lunch and dinner fare in a hip atmosphere. The menu changes regularly to highlight the freshest ingredients.

Catering Services

  • Working class hipsters advocating for safe working conditions
  • Snacks for bridge club
  • The bourgoisie's bicentennial Caturday party
